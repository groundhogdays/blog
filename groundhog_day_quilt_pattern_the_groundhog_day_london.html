Why Do We Celebrate Groundhog Day?

Groundhog Day is a North American holiday celebrated annually on February 2nd. The day is associated with the belief that a groundhog — most famously Punxsutawney Phil — can predict the onset of spring. According to tradition, if the groundhog sees its shadow, there will be six more weeks of winter.
